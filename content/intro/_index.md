CoLab(Cola Laboratory,可乐实验室) was founded on June 12, 2016. With strong expertise in human-centric image and video understanding—including human segmentation, pose estimation, attribute prediction, and re-identification—we now focus on integrating AI with advanced platforms such as drones, embodied agents, and satellite remote sensing.
